How to I get an illustration made?

I work with your local photography studio. They will take the base photo, submit it to us where we will create the artwork. Once completed we will send back to your photographer for printing.

What if my local photographer doesn't use Murphy Studios ?

You need to talk to your photographer ....show him this website and ask him/her contact Murphy Studio. Its a quick and easy connection ....seriously, it's a Win-Win for everyone!

What are the sizes of the final output?

The sizes are simular to what you could normally get for portraits ....from wallet to poster. Discuss with your local photo studio.
